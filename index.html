Data Privacy Protocol

By design, ResuMatch Core operates entirely on your machine. Your resume data and Job Description text are processed in volatile browser memory and are never uploaded to a cloud for storage or training.

The "Heuristic Engine" lives in your local session. Closing the tab immediately purges all buffered data. We do not use third-party tracking pixels.

How does the Heuristic Sync work?

Instead of relying on remote AI servers, we use a local logic engine that parses text patterns, calculates keyword density, and applies a multi-vector semantic mapping algorithm entirely within your browser's JS engine.

Is my resume data secure?

Absolute Zero Transmission. Your files are processed locally. No external APIs, no databases, and no tracking. Once you close this tab, the "Vector Buffer" is entirely destroyed.

What is a "Career Vector"?

A vector is a mathematical direction. In our context, it represents the specific strength of a skill or experience block. We map these vectors against the Job Description to ensure your "Signal" matches the recruiter's "Receiver."
